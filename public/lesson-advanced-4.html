Advanced Lesson 4

Web3 and the Future of the Decentralized Web

Lesson Overview

This lesson covers the concept of Web3, how it differs from Web2, and what it means for the future of internet applications, user privacy, and data ownership.

Topics Covered:

  • Web1 vs. Web2 vs. Web3
  • How decentralized applications (dApps) work
  • Benefits and challenges of Web3 adoption
  • Real-world use cases

Assignment:

Write a short essay on how Web3 could reshape industries like social media, finance, or e-commerce. Mention at least one existing Web3 application.
