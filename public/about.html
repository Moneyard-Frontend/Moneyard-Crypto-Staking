About Moneyard

Your crypto-powered path to passive income, savings, and financial education

Who We Are

Moneyard is a modern crypto staking and micro-investment platform with a growing financial literacy and online learning arm. Designed for mainstream investors, beginners, and everyday users, our mission is to simplify wealth building through crypto earning, high-yield saving, and education—especially in mobile-first markets across Africa and beyond.

Our Core Offerings

  • Crypto staking (TRC20/BEP20)
  • High-yield savings accounts
  • Micro-investing tools
  • Online courses + accredited certificates

Why Choose Moneyard?

  • Beginner-friendly and mobile-optimized interface
  • Fast profits + passive income through crypto
  • Financial education to support long-term growth
  • Supports crypto, mobile money, and card deposits
  • Admin-verified deposits and OTP-secured withdrawals

Our Vision

To be the go-to platform for secure crypto growth and financial education—starting in Africa, expanding globally. Whether you're saving, staking, investing, or learning, Moneyard is your trusted partner on the journey to financial freedom.

Business Focus

  • Industry: Fintech, Crypto, Digital Learning
  • Audience: First-time users, mobile-savvy investors, students
  • Platform: Web + Mobile App (Crypto-integrated)
  • Innovation: Web3 tools, micro-investment plans, online education
  • Focus: Africa-first, expanding to global emerging markets
