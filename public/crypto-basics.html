6-Week Financial Literacy & Micro-Investment Course

Week 1: Introduction to Financial Literacy

Learn the basics of money management, budgeting, and financial goals.

Week 2: Budgeting & Saving Techniques

Explore techniques to control spending and build emergency savings.

Week 3: Understanding Banking & Credit

Understand bank accounts, credit scores, loans, and financial institutions.

Week 4: Introduction to Investing

Get familiar with stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and risk vs reward.

Week 5: Micro-Investing Tools & Apps

Discover platforms like Acorns, Robinhood, and other micro-investment tools.

Week 6: Building Wealth & Staying Consistent

Learn how to stay committed and automate your path to long-term wealth.
