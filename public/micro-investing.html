Financial Literacy & Micro Investments Course

Lesson 1: Introduction to Financial Literacy

Financial literacy is the ability to understand and effectively use various financial skills, including personal financial management, budgeting, and investing.

Lesson 2: Importance of Saving

Saving is a critical part of financial health. Learn how to manage your money and build your savings to secure your future.

Lesson 3: Understanding Investments

Investing can help you grow your wealth. In this lesson, we'll explore different types of investments such as stocks, bonds, and crypto assets.

Lesson 4: Risk Management in Investments

Every investment involves risk. Learn how to assess and manage risks when making financial decisions, especially in the volatile crypto market.

Lesson 5: Building an Investment Portfolio

A diversified portfolio helps reduce risk. Learn how to build a portfolio that balances risk and reward to achieve long-term financial goals.

Lesson 6: Introduction to Micro Investments

Micro investments allow individuals to invest small amounts of money. Learn how to start investing with just a few dollars and watch your wealth grow over time.
