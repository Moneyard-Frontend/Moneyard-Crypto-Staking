Advanced Lesson 2

Smart Contract Auditing

Lesson Overview

Learn the fundamentals of smart contract auditing. This lesson explains static analysis tools, common Solidity vulnerabilities, and the steps security firms follow during audits.

Topics Covered:

  • How smart contracts are audited
  • Reentrancy and overflow vulnerabilities
  • Audit reports and how to interpret them
  • Best practices for writing secure contracts

Assignment:

Find and analyze a real smart contract audit report online. Summarize the key issues found and how they were fixed.
