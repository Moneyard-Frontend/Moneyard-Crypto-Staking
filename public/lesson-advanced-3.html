Advanced Lesson 3

Blockchain Governance Models

Lesson Overview

This lesson explores governance models in blockchain systems, how communities make decisions, and the differences between on-chain and off-chain governance mechanisms.

Topics Covered:

  • What is blockchain governance?
  • Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)
  • Voting systems and token-based decision-making
  • Case studies: Ethereum vs. Cardano governance

Assignment:

Compare two blockchain projects with different governance models. Identify pros and cons of each and share your opinion on the more sustainable approach.
