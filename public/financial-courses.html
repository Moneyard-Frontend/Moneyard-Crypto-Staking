Lesson 3: Emergency Funds

Start building a safety net for unexpected costs—aim for 3–6 months of expenses.

Credit impacts your ability to borrow. Learn about credit scores, reports, and responsible usage.

Lesson 5: Savings & Compounding

Lesson 6: Investing Basics

Understand risk vs. reward, and different types of assets: stocks, bonds, crypto, real estate.

Lesson 7: Diversification

Diversify to reduce risk: mix of assets, industries, time horizons.

Lesson 8: Crypto Investing

Learn the basics of crypto, wallets, network fees, risks and rewards.

Start small, grow big. Apps and tools now allow you to invest with as little as $1.
